About Us

Zen Spaces is a rental property management company that helps investors grow their income property portfolio revenue.
We maximize your return by creating short, medium, and long-term listings for your property, and then fill your property with the highest yielding and professional renter.

History

Zen Spaces was founded with a vision to revolutionize the short-term rental industry by providing a unique partnership model with landlords. Established with the recognition of growing demand for Airbnb and other short-term rental platforms, we saw an opportunity to help property owners maximize their profits.

With years of experience in the real estate sectors, we identified the challenges landlords faced in managing and marketing their properties effectively. Thus, Zen Spaces was born as a solution-driven company, offering comprehensive services to create and optimize vacation listings on behalf of landlords.

Vision

Our vision at Zen Spaces is to redefine the vacation rental experience for both landlords and guests. We strive to become the leading partner for landlords worldwide, empowering them to effortlessly monetize their properties and achieve long-term success in the short-term rental market. We put rental properties on vacation sites like AirBNB, VRBO etc.

By leveraging cutting-edge technology, industry expertise, and a customer-centric approach, we aim to create memorable and seamless stays for guests while maximizing the financial potential of every property.

Mission

At Zen Spaces, our mission is to be the catalyst that unlocks the full potential of your property. We are committed to delivering exceptional results for landlords through our innovative profit-sharing model and comprehensive management services. By leveraging our expertise in listing optimization, pricing strategies, and guest communication, we aim to attract a steady stream of high-quality guests who appreciate the unique offerings of your property.

Our dedicated team of professionals is passionate about providing impeccable customer service, ensuring that both landlords and guests have a stress-free experience throughout their journey with Zen Spaces. We are dedicated to transparency, trust, and long-term partnerships, with a focus on driving sustainable growth and delivering outstanding value to our clients. Together, let's unlock the full potential of your property and create unforgettable experiences for guests.

FAQs

If you have any questions, please read the Frequently Asked Questions.

Provided we have photographs for your property and all the relevant details required from the landlord (including but not limited to: government ID, fully completed House Manual and listing deposit), your listing can go live in 48 hours on AirBNB or less.

If your photography is scheduled, it can take up to 72 hours for the photogrpaher to edit and send us the final photographs, after which the listing will go live in 48 hours.

For channels besides AirBNB, the listing can take around 24-48 hours for approval from HomeAway and another 3-4 weeks for Booking.com due to their independent approval process.

Our fully trained housekeepers will do a full service clean of your property, make the beds up, and replenish amenities such as toilet rolls, shampoo, and shower gel, tea, coffee, sugar and even olive oil.

Trust us, when we are done with your home, your guests will give you a stars rating.

Additional charges a landlord may be liable for but are not limited to include:

Any housekeeping services organised after a landlord's stay in the flat.
Any maintenance works carried by our handymen including giving access to a third party maintenance company or delivering requested items to the property during a guest's stay.
Any third party maintenance company charges and co-ordination charges.

Our Management fee varies depending on the type of property we're managing, the revenue potential of the property as well as the volume of properties under negotiatoin.

Management fees are agreed with each host individually. Tell us about your property and our account management team will be in cotact to answer any quesitons you may have.

Our housekeeping team are trained to identify any signs of obvious and substantial damage to your home, and then alert this issue to our Account Management team. We then proceed on a case-by-case basis, depending on the severity of the damage.

However, we would always advise that hosts should expect some light wear and tear, and encourage you to look into short letting insurance options(we can help you with this). In addition, we asks all our landlords to lock away items of value that you want to protect.
